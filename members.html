Our Team

Just as a diverse human microbiota promotes good health, a diverse work group fosters team growth and productivity.

We believe that:
  • A learning and working environment where all members feel supported (...sense of belonging, respected, have a voice) produces the strongest research (e.g increased output, bigger impact, and greater innovation).

  • The team benefits from the different perspectives and innovative approaches that diverse members (ethnic, race, gender, age, disciplinary and academic background) bring to the group.

  • Inclusive communication and collaboration are fundamental for induvial and team success.
