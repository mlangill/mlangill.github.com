Welcome to The Langille Lab at Dalhousie University, Halifax, Nova Scotia, Canada.

Our lab is interested in understanding the role of the microbiome on various diseases by developing novel bioinformatic methods for use in both animal and human studies.

We are always looking for new research collaborations and opportunities so please don't hesitate to contact us.
