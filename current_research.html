Current Research

Human Microbiome

Currently, I am a post-doc funded by the Canadian Human Microbiome Intiative on "Modeling and mapping microbial diversity and function with marker genes, genomes and metagenomes" led by Dr. Ford Doolittle with direct supervision by co-PI Dr. Robert Beiko. In collaboration with Rob Knight and Curtis Huttenhower, we have been developing, PICRUST, software to infer functional information from a metagenome based on 16S data only.

Ocean Metagenomics

During my first post-doc I was a member of the iSEEM research group supported by the Moore Foundation led by Dr. Jonathan Eisen for improving computational methods for metagenomics data.
